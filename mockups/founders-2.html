From the founders

Why we built Nulle.

Between us, we’ve spent 30 years building enterprise software — one of us coming from mathematics, the other from electrical engineering, both writing production code since 2011. One of us has spent that time designing cloud architectures, obsessing over delivery, security, and availability at enterprise scale. The other has designed, built, and shipped enterprise iOS and mobile apps to the App Store, reaching thousands of users. We’ve seen what real systems demand, and we’ve seen what they’re becoming.

We didn’t start Nulle to compete with the big platforms. We started it because we watched the most powerful technology of our lifetime get handed to a handful of companies — and everyone else was told to rent it back.

That’s not a future we wanted to build toward. We believe intelligence should be owned, not leased. Private by default. Local where it matters. Transparent about what it does and doesn’t do.

Nulle is our answer. A small company, building real tools and real partnerships with people who want a different relationship with their technology.

If any of that resonates — you’re in the right place.

Written from Atlanta, Georgia · by way of New York.
