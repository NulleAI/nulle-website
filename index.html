The landscape today

Most AI asks you to make a trade-off.

Send your data to the cloud to get intelligence back. Trust someone else’s infrastructure with your most sensitive information. Hope that “privacy settings” actually work.

The trade-off
  • Your data leaves your environment
  • You depend on someone else’s infrastructure
  • Privacy is a setting you toggle
  • You need internet to think
  • Your information trains someone else’s model
What we help you build
  • Your data never leaves your machines
  • You own and control the full stack
  • Privacy is the foundation, not a checkbox
  • Intelligence works even offline
  • Your data serves you — only you
